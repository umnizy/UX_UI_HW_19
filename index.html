USER EMPATHY

QUALITATIVE INTERVIEWS

When conducting qualitative interviews, I start by setting a goal, what do I want to learn from the user? I prepare a list of questions and use the Five Ws to learn as much as I can. I also keep in mind that it's important to understand what users need, hope and wish for in the products I design.
